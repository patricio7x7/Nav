Core Conjugation Patterns
How to useDon't try to memorize rules — use this as a lookup when you encounter forms in the stories. After enough reading, the patterns become instinct. Focus first on: ~masu, ~masen, ~mashita, ~te kudasai, and the casual ~ta / ~nai forms.
desu — "to be" / "it is"

Attach to nouns and na-adjectives. The backbone of polite speech.

FormRomajiExample
Present polite~ desuGakusei desu.I'm a student.
Present negative~ ja nai desuGakusei ja nai desu.I'm not a student.
Past polite~ deshitaIi tenki deshita.It was nice weather.
Past negative~ ja nakatta desuMuzukashiku ja nakatta.It wasn't difficult.
Casual present~ daSou da.That's right.
Casual negative~ ja naiSore ja nai.That's not it.
~masu verbs — polite present / future

The standard polite form. Use with people you don't know well. Example verb: taberu (to eat)

FormEndingExample
Present / Future~masuTabemasu.I eat / I will eat.
Negative~masenTabemasen.I don't / won't eat.
Past~mashitaTabemashita.I ate.
Past negative~masen deshitaTabemasen deshita.I didn't eat.
Want to~tai desuTabetai desu.I want to eat.
Don't want to~taku naiTabetaku nai.I don't want to eat.
Please do~te kudasaiTabete kudasai.Please eat.
Currently doing~te imasuTabete imasu.I am eating.

Change the final ~i. Example: oishii (delicious)

FormPatternExample

Section 01 · Story 01
Hajimemashite, Tanaka-san — Nice to Meet You
~480 words · Meeting a stranger · Café · Polite + casual mix
narration

Kenta wa koohii shoppu ni imasu. Hitori de suwatte, koohii wo nonde imasu. Kyou wa nichiyoubi da. Hima da.

Kenta is at a coffee shop. Sitting alone, drinking coffee. Today is Sunday. He's free.

Kyou

Sumimasen, koko ni suwatte mo ii desu ka?

Excuse me, is it okay if I sit here?

Kenta

Aa, hai, douzo douzo.

Oh yes, go ahead, please.

Kyou

Arigatou gozaimasu. Kono mise, itsumo ippai desu ne.

Thank you. This place is always packed, isn't it.

Kenta

Sou desu ne. Nichiyoubi wa tokuni hito ga ooi desu.

It really is. On Sundays there are especially a lot of people.

narration

Futari wa sukoshi dake damatte imashita. Soshite Kyou ga mata hanashimashita.

The two sat in silence for just a moment. Then Kyou spoke again.

Kyou

Ano... watashi, Suzuki Kyou to iimasu. Yoroshiku onegaishimasu.

Um... my name is Suzuki Kyou. Nice to meet you.

Kenta

Aa! Hajimemashite! Boku wa Tanaka Kenta desu. Yoroshiku!

Oh! Nice to meet you! I'm Tanaka Kenta. Good to meet you!

Kyou

Tanaka-san wa, itsumo koko ni kimasu ka?

Tanaka-san, do you come here often?

Kenta

Sou desu ne... maishuu kuru to omoimasu. Koohii ga oishii kara. Suzuki-san wa?

Let me think... I think I come every week. The coffee is good. What about Suzuki-san?

Kyou

Watashi wa hajimete desu. Tomodachi ni oshiete moratte... Chikaku ni sunde imasu ka?

This is my first time. A friend told me about it... Do you live nearby?

Kenta

Ee, aruite gofun gurai desu. Chikai desu yo. Suzuki-san wa?

Yeah, it's about five minutes on foot. It's close! What about you?

Kyou

Watashi wa chikatetsu de juugofun gurai. Sou tooku nai kedo ne.

About fifteen minutes by subway. It's not that far though.

Kenta

Aa, sou desu ka. Ano... shitsurei desu ga, donna oshigoto wo sarete imasu ka?

Oh really. Um... sorry to ask, but what kind of work do you do?

Kyou

Aa, watashi wa dezainaa desu. Furi no. Tanaka-san wa?

Oh, I'm a designer. Freelance. What about you, Tanaka-san?

Kenta

Sugoi! Watashi wa futsuu no kaishain desu. IT no kaisha desu.

That's impressive! I'm at a regular company. An IT company.

Kyou

Ii desu ne! Isogashii desu ka, mainichi?

Nice! Are you busy every day?

Kenta

Maa... tokidoki sugoku isogashii desu. Demo kyou wa yasumi desu kara, yukkuri shite imasu. Suzuki-san mo hima?

Well... sometimes really busy. But today's a day off so I'm taking it slow. Are you free too, Suzuki-san?

Kyou

Hai, kyou wa hima desu yo. Etto, Kenta-san, youkattara — mata issho ni koohii demo nomimasen ka?

Yes, I'm free today. Uh, Kenta-san, if you're up for it — want to grab coffee together again sometime?

Kenta

Ii desu ne! Zehi. Renraku saki, oshiete moraemasu ka?

Sounds great! Definitely. Could you give me your contact info?

Kyou

Mochiron! Hai, douzo.

Of course! Here you go.

narration

Kyou wa sumaaho wo dashimashita. Kenta mo sumaaho wo dashimashita. Futari wa renraku saki wo koukanshimashita. Atarashii tomodachi ga dekimashita.

Kyou took out their phone. Kenta did too. They exchanged contact info. A new friendship had begun.

Resutoran de — At the Restaurant
~520 words · Ordering food · Paying · Polite service language
narration

Mika to Hiroshi wa resutoran no mae ni imasu. Ima wa yoru no shichiji han desu. Futari wa onaka ga suite imasu.

Mika and Hiroshi are outside a restaurant. It's 7:30 in the evening. They're both hungry.

Mika

Hiroshi, koko de ii?

Hiroshi, is here okay?

Hiroshi

Un, ii ne. Omise, kirei da shi... haitte miyo!

Yeah, looks good. The place is clean too... let's go in!

narration

Futari wa naka ni hairimashita. Sutaffu ga sugu ni kite kuremashita.

The two went inside. A staff member came over right away.

Sutaffu

Irasshaimase! Nan mei sama desu ka?

Welcome! How many in your party?

Hiroshi

Futari desu.

Two.

Sutaffu

Kashikomarimashita. Kochira e douzo.

Certainly. Right this way.

narration

Seki ni tsukimashita. Menyu wo moraimashita.

They were seated. Menus arrived.

Mika

Nani ni suru? Watashi, ramen ni shiyou ka na...

What are you getting? I'm thinking ramen maybe...

Hiroshi

Boku wa... hmm. Kono teishoku ga yosasou da ne. Sakana to gohan to miso shiru. Ii na.

I'll go with... hmm. This set meal looks good. Fish and rice and miso soup. Nice.

Mika

Aa, sore mo oishisou! Demo watashi wa kyou niku ga tabetai naa.

Oh that looks good too! But today I really want meat.

Hiroshi

Jaa, tonkatsu teishoku wa? Mite, koko ni aru yo.

Then what about the tonkatsu set? Look, it's right here.

Mika

Aa, ii! Sore ni suru. Hiroshi wa nomimono wa?

Oh nice! I'll go with that. What about drinks, Hiroshi?

Hiroshi

Biiru nomitai kedo... kuruma da kara na. Ocha de ii ya.

I want a beer but... I drove, so. Tea is fine.

Mika

Aa, sou da ne. Watashi mo ocha ni suru.

Oh right, good call. I'll have tea too.

narration

Sutaffu ga modorimashita.

The staff member came back.

Sutaffu

Gochuumon wa, okimari deshita ka?

Are you ready to order?

Hiroshi

Hai. Sakana teishoku to tonkatsu teishoku, hitotsu zutsu onegaishimasu. Nomimono wa ocha wo futatsu.

Yes. One fish set and one tonkatsu set please. And two teas.

Sutaffu

Arigatou gozaimasu. Shoushou omachi kudasai.

Thank you. Just a moment please.

narration

Juugofun gurai tachimashita. Ryouri ga kimashita.

About fifteen minutes passed. The food arrived.

Mika

Waa, oishisou! Itadakimasu!

Wow, that looks delicious! Let's eat!

Hiroshi

Itadakimasu! ...mm! Kore, hontou ni oishii ne.

Let's eat! ...mm! This is really good isn't it.

Mika

Sou sou! Tonkatsu ga saku saku shite iru! Koko, mata kiyou.

Right, right! The tonkatsu is so crispy! Let's come back here.

Hiroshi

Boku mo! Tsugi wa tomodachi mo tsurete kiyou ka.

Me too! Next time let's bring our friends.

narration

Shokuji ga owatte, Hiroshi wa sutaffu wo yobimashita.

The meal finished. Hiroshi called the staff over.

Hiroshi

Sumimasen! Okaikei onegaishimasu.

Excuse me! The bill please.

Sutaffu

Hai, shoushou omachi kudasai... Gosen happyaku en ni narimasu.

Of course, one moment... That comes to 5,800 yen.

Hiroshi

Jaa, boku ga harau yo.
